# Executive Brief Skill

## When To Use
- Audience is leadership, product heads, or non-technical stakeholders.
- User asks for concise summary and recommendation.

## Workflow
1. Start with one-sentence recommendation.
2. Present top 3 decision-driving facts.
3. List tradeoffs and timing considerations.
4. End with a concrete next action and owner.

## Output Format
## Recommendation
## Why Now
## Top Risks and Mitigations
## Decision Needed Today

## Quality Rules
- Keep it concise and non-jargony.
- Do not hide uncertainty; state confidence explicitly.
